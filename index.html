Our Story

Born from the
Depths of Myth

House of Hades is more than a venue — it's a passage into another realm. Nestled in the shadows between worlds, we offer an immersive experience where ancient Greek myth meets modern indulgence. Sip hand-crafted cocktails named for the Olympians, feast on Mediterranean-inspired plates, and let the atmosphere carry you to the banks of the Styx.

Part pub, part restaurant, entirely a chill-out sanctuary. Whether you come for an intimate evening or a grand gathering, the underworld opens its gates for you.
